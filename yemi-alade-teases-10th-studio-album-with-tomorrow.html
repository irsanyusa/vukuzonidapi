Yemi Alade Teases 10th studio album with 'Tomorrow'

September 12, 2024
blog

Produced by Yasso, this vibrant and positive track is the first single from Yemi’s upcoming tenth album, set for release in 2024.

Speaking about the release, Yemi Alade shared, “Tomorrow is a timeless piece that honors the aspirations of the future and the ongoing pursuit of turning them into achievements. This is a dedication to all those envisioning brighter days, serving as an anthem for champions.”

ADVERTISEMENT

Yemi Alade is excited to offer this new body of work, revealing that the upcoming album will be a blend of different genres designed to appeal to music lovers worldwide.

“I created this album with the sole purpose of creating music I grew up loving and the variety I have grown to love now. I was inspired by the core African music of Highlife and different genres that got us excited as Africans,” she explained.

Starting the year 2024 on a high note, Yemi Alade performed at the opening ceremony of the 2024 Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON).

Her upcoming album will further enhance her stellar discography and global influence. She is also set to headline a show in Zenith, Paris – La Villette, France on September 21, 2024, alongside her African Train Band.
