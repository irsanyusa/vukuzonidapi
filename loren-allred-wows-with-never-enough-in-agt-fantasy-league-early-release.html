America’s Got Talent: Fantasy League has shared an early release video from next week’s new episode, featuring former Britain’s Got Talent contestant Loren Allred. In the clip, Allred takes the stage to sing “Never Enough” from The Greatest Showman.

Loren Allred Sings ‘Never Enough’ on AGT: Fantasy League

It should surprise no one that Allred is on Simon Cowell’s team for Fantasy League. Not only did Allred reach the Finals of BGT in 2022, but she’s the voice behind a song from one of his favorite movies, The Greatest Showman.

In the early release clip, Cowell can be seen talking to Allred before her performance, suggesting she do a new take on the song for Fantasy League. Allred called this version a “step up” from the original, in which she would be “testing my vocal range.”

As demonstrated in the clip, Allred totally slayed the new arrangement. The performance ended with a long, drawn-out high note that wowed the judges and had the audience cheering. The entire panel gave Allred a standing ovation.

What Did the Judges Think of Her Performance?

“Wow. Wow,” Howie Mandel said after Allred’s performance. “I mean, that’s amazing that such great talent is so not known and behind the scenes. So wonderful of you to bring it forward and introduce it to the world.”

Heidi Klum said everyone there was “lucky” to have been able to witness Allred’s performance, adding, “It was just absolutely beautiful.” Mel B said she was “lost for words,” adding that it’s “just amazing what you can do.”

Cowell described Allred’s performance as “honestly spectacular.” He shared his hope that the audience will vote Allred through to the Semifinals. Cowell called Allred an “extraordinarily good artist.”

You can watch the new episode of AGT: Fantasy League Monday night on NBC to find out if Allred makes it through to the next round of the competition.
