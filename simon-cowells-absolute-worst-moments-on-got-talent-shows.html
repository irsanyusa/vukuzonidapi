Simon Cowell is known for his sharp tongue and no-nonsense attitude when it comes to judging talent shows. His brutally honest feedback has made him both feared and respected by aspiring artists on various talent shows, most notably Got Talent. However, behind the success and charm lies a controversial figure whose sharp tongue and blunt remarks have sparked widespread debate.

In this article, we delve into Cowell’s worst moments on Got Talent, shedding light on instances where his judging style has crossed the line into being mean, rude, and downright controversial.

Simon Cowell’s Worst Moments on Got Talent

1. Calling Three Girls “The Singing Trolls”

During Series Three of Britain’s Got Talent, The Singing Souls, a singing trio consisting of Louise, Tasha, and Hannah, had an unfortunate audition experience. Cowell humorously misheard their name as “The Singing Trolls” and their out-of-tune singing led to quick buzzers from all the judges. Hannah responded angrily to the criticism, engaging in arguments with Cowell, to which the judge did not back down. Ultimately, the audition stage sent The Singing Souls home, thereby making it one of Cowell’s worst moments on the show.

2. Cowell Calling A Dancer’s Moves “Useless”

During Series Two of BGT, dancer David Williams faced a harsh critique from Cowell. After performing a routine to “How Do You Do” by Cascada, Cowell described his dance moves as “completely and utterly useless,” prompting Williams to run off the stage and direct profanity towards the judges and the audience.

3. Argument With Mel B on AGT

Cowell had a heated exchange with fellow judge Mel B during an episode of AGT. Mel B threw water at Cowell after he made a sarcastic comment about one of the contestants. The incident sparked controversy and generated considerable media attention, highlighting the tension and conflict that can arise between judges on talent shows.

4. Cowell Tells BGT Judges his AGT Castmates Are Smarter

Cowell was asked to choose between his judging panels on AGT and BGT. In response, he controversially stated, that his AGT castmates are more “intelligent,” basically calling his fellow BGT judges dumb. This moment highlighted a perceived arrogance and a lack of respect, earning it a place among Cowell’s worst moments as a judge.

5. Cowell Said Marcelito Pomoy’s Performance Was Predictable

During MArcelito Pomoy’s last performance on AGT: The Champions 2, he sang “Beauty And The Beast,” a song that failed to impress Cowell. While the judges mostly gave positive comments, Cowell voiced his disapproval of Pomoy’s performance, calling it “predictable.” Both the panel and the audience responded with disapproval to his critique.
