A huge part of Gwen Stefani’s life is the love she has for her children Kingston, 14, Zuma, 12, and Apollo, 6. When she began dating Blake Shelton in 2015, the kids became a huge part of his life too. Blake recently gushed about becoming a stepfather to Gwen’s kids after he proposed to her in October.

The country superstar spoke on the KFROG radio show “The Ride with Kimo & Heather” about his daddy duties. You can listen to the full radio interview here.

Blake Shelton Loves Being A Stepfather To Gwen Stefani’s Kids

“There’s definitely nothing easy about it. I don’t know if it’s as hard or harder or not as hard as being an actual, biological parent, you know? I have a stepfather in my life who is one of my heroes,” Blake said in the interview about fatherhood. “I love my stepfather and he’s like a father to me, so I have a good inspiration in my life on how to do this and the kind of stepdad I want to be.”

Blake’s biological father Dick Shelton passed away in 2012. Blake canceled tour dates during this time to be there for his family. His mother Dorothy is married to Mike Shackleford, Blake’s stepdad.

“I take it very serious, but I also have a blast with it,” he said during the interview. “I don’t take it so serious that I’m not enjoying this time because I really am, especially now that we’re five years into this thing. I can’t imagine my life without these kids now.”

Gwen Gushed About Blake’s Daddy Skills In The Past

During an appearance on The Today Show in 2019, Gwen gushed about Blake’s parenting skills. “He is a good dad, actually,” she said. “He’s been helping me out a lot. I literally get to the point where I’m like, ‘You gotta get home—I need help!’ It’s hard. I got three boys.”

But Blake has had to find the perfect balance of being a parent and a friend to Gwen’s sons. “That’s a scary moment for me because it’s one thing for me to be with the kids all the time and be their buddy, but you do have to consider after a while that they start to listen to things that you say. There’s a lot of responsibility that comes with that,” Blake said in an interview.

We cannot wait to see Blake and Gwen walk down the aisle. Gwen’s kids “will have a large part” in the wedding between the two ‘Voice’ coaches. “Gwen’s sons are really solid, sweet kids, and they’re so excited for Blake and Gwen to get married,” a friend close to the couple told People. 

Blake Also Spoke About His Hilarious Super Bowl Ad With His Future Wife

The best part of the Super Bowl was seeing Blake and Gwen star in a hilarious commercial with their fellow ‘Voice’ coach Adam Levine. Though the commercial was a parody of their relationship that proves opposites attract, Blake is still enamored that he is engaged to Gwen.

“People to this day—and I’m one of them, by the way—just can’t wrap their head around the fact that Gwen Stefani is my girlfriend, now my fiancé,” the “God’s Country,” singer said during the radio interview. “It is ridiculous. I admit that…I will admit it. I don’t know what she’s thinking.”

Blake doesn’t give himself enough credit. On top of winning The Voice seven times and having a plethora of smash hit songs, he is an awesome stepdad and future husband. Check out the video below to find out more on how Blake became such a huge superstar.
