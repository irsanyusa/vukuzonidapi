Renowned Victoria’s Secret runway model, Jessica White, bares her soul as she unveils the genuine reason behind her split with popular host Nick Cannon. While their relationship spanned several years, from 2015 to 2020, White sheds light on the true factors that led to their separation, providing an intimate glimpse into their personal journey.

Jessica White Revealed Why She Split Up With Nick Cannon

In an open and honest revelation, White shares her perspective on Instagram, providing insight into her emotions. White bravely confronts her own healing process, acknowledging that her personal journey of growth extends beyond her time with Cannon.

“I want to set the record straight about why our relationship ended. It was never about pride or lack thereof,” White said. “There were deeper issues at play, and I feel compelled to share the truth.”

With a desire to provide clarity, White addresses her emotional state and the underlying dynamics of their connection. She emphasizes that her decision to part ways with Cannon was not driven by external factors but rather stemmed from profound internal struggles that affected their relationship on a deeper level.

Furthermore, White emphasizes her unwavering self-acceptance and the importance of self-love amidst the complexities of fame and success. She recognizes the shared history of pain and brokenness that she and Cannon carried from their childhoods, acknowledging that their inability to love each other fully stemmed from their own individual journeys of healing.

Jessica White Opens Up About Nick Cannon’s Behavior

White expresses her feelings of instability, revealing that Cannon’s tendency to put other women on a pedestal contributed to her emotional turmoil. These revelations offer a glimpse into the complex circumstances surrounding their breakup and the emotional challenges faced by White during that time.

“I was going through still my hormonal changes, and we were about to start in vitro. So when I came out, people thought I was this homewrecker,” White said. “I was bullied for months with that whole situation. I still tried to be nice about it. We didn’t break up right away; we tried to work things out.”

Cannon, known for his prolific fatherhood, has 12 children with six different women. Almost adding to that number in 2020, White experienced a heartbreaking miscarriage. Shortly after, their relationship came to an end, partially due to Cannon’s announcement of expecting a child with Brittany Bell.
