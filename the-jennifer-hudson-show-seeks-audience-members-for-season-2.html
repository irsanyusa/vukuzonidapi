The Jennifer Hudson Show is gearing up for Season 2, and fans can request tickets now to be in the audience when filming begins next month. The talk show kicked off last fall, and it was renewed for a second season earlier this year.

The Jennifer Hudson Show Seeks Audience Members

Earlier this week, The Jennifer Hudson Show posted an announcement on Instagram letting fans know that they can now request tickets to attend a taping of Season 2. The caption reads, “It’s time to let us look you upside the head!”

Fans can request their tickets on the show’s website. Currently, tickets are available for shows on September 14, 18, 20, 21, 25, 27, and 28. You can click on the date you want and fill out your information.

“The sooner you make a selection, the sooner we’ll see you on the dance floor. (Yes! There’s a JHud audience dance floor!),” reads the ticket page.

Keep in mind that, according to the FAQs, everyone attending the show must be at least 14 years old. Minors must be accompanied by an adult and have proof of age. Information about the show’s guests will not be revealed until the day of the show.

Hudson Celebrated Her Renewal in January

In January of this year, The Jennifer Hudson Show was renewed for a second season. The host got emotional as she shared the news with her audience and spoke about how much the talk show means to her.

“Thank you all for giving me your hearts,” she said. “I love y’all so much. I love you so much. I really do. Thank you for letting me into your homes, into your lives. You bless us just the same.”

Since the show premiered last September, Hudson has welcomed a number of celebrity guests, including talent show stars such as Simon Cowell, Ken Jeong, Nicole Scherzinger, and Blake Shelton. She’s even performed with some of her guests, such as The Masked Singer winner Amber Riley and fellow American Idol star Adam Lambert.
