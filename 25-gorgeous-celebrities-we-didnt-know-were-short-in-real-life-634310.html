20 Gorgeous Celebrities We Didnt Know Were Short in Real Life

July 09, 2024
blog

We never really know how tall our favorite celebrities are. In movies, they use special boxes to make shorter actors and actresses look taller, and even male stars don’t shy away from wearing platform or heeled shoes. Maybe it’s because of these tricks, the way they carry themselves, or the fact that they’re “stars”, that celebs often seem taller than they really are. However, they’re still human, and just like the rest of us, they come in different heights.
