Student life in Prague - Live &amp; Study

August 15, 2024
blog

10. 9. 2021 | Student Life

Prague is a city of dreams. It is the cultural and historical center of the entire country. There are hundreds of cinemas, art galleries, music halls, clubs, sports facilities, and parks that lay on top of each other. Most of the universities and their colleges are situated right in the very core of the city and are easily reachable from every direction by public transport. You will never experience boredom in Prague. Correctly choosing how you want to spend your free time is going to be difficult because of the variety of options that Prague has to offer.

If you’re someone who likes to meet new people and hang out in artsy cafes and pubs then Vršovice and Vinohrady are going to be your go-to places. Other than going to the pubs you can also go paddle boarding with your friends and discover the beautiful city from a different perspective.

Cinemas and food

If you are a movie enthusiast who doesn’t like to miss out on the latest independent production then you will certainly find a satisfying refuge in one of the twelve art cinemas. If you enjoy any type of music and are open to discovering new brands and styles, then you are going to have unlimited options in this city. Right from food to music to doing fun activities, Prague has everything you need to offer. Not a day in this city will be boring.

How to stay focused in Prague

Although student life in Prague is not just about having fun and partying with your friends. To tone down your energy you can always find quiet me-time and visit a museum or a library. The Municipal Library of Prague has 42 branches around the city, where you can not only find a section for English and Czech literature but also other foreign languages. You will find literature ranging from poetry to comic books.

Make sure to not be lazy when you’re staying in Prague and take full advantage of all the things that it has to offer. I am telling you, this city will never disappoint you, or bore you!
