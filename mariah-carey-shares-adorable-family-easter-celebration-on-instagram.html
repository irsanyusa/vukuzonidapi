Mariah Carey has always been known for her powerful vocals and flashy style, but behind the scenes, she is a dedicated mother who loves spending time with her family. Recently, the superstar celebrated Easter with her 11-year-old twins, Monroe and Moroccan, and shared the heartwarming moment on social media.

Mariah Carey Spends Easter With Twins

While some celebrities might use Easter as an opportunity to flaunt their wealth or status, Carey showed the world that the holiday is really about spending time with loved ones and expressing gratitude. Her Easter celebration was a fun and heartwarming moment for the singer and her family.

In a video on her Instagram account, Carey is happily spending time with her children and a group of friends on Easter Sunday. Carey and her kids were able to meet the highlight of the video, a cute and cuddly bunny rabbit.

The Easter celebration is just one of many family moments that Carey has shared with her fans on social media. In September 2022, she posted photos of herself and her children at an amusement park, with Carey hilariously admitting that she made the mistake of wearing heels for the occasion.

Despite her high-profile career and status as a celebrity, Carey actively co-parents her children with her ex-husband, Nick Cannon. In interviews, Cannon has spoken about how much he admires Carey’s kindness and positive attitude, and the two have also remained close friends even after their divorce.

Mariah Carey’s Easter Bunny Surprise for Her Twins

Carey’s love for her children is no secret, and the superstar singer always finds a way to make holidays special for her family. This year’s Easter celebration was no exception, as Carey shared another heartwarming moment with her twins

In another video on her Instagram account, Carey got a visit by an Easter bunny who is carrying a golden egg. The bunny then leads Carey to her living room, where her children are sound asleep while watching cartoons.

Carey’s Easter celebration with her family is a reminder of the importance of cherishing the moments we have with our loved ones. In a world where fame and fortune often take center stage, Carey’s dedication to her family is a breath of fresh air and a true inspiration.
