So You Think You Can Dance judge JoJo Siwa seems to have confirmed that she’s dating Avery Cyrus in a new TikTok video posted this week. Siwa’s ex-girlfriend Kylie Prew confirmed that they had split last month.

JoJo Siwa Confirms She’s Dating Avery Cyrus

Siwa got back together with her on and off girlfriend Kylie Prew earlier this year. However, Prew confirmed in August that they were no longer together. Fans began to speculate that Siwa could be dating fellow TikTok star Avery Cyrus.

At first, Siwa called Cyrus “a really good friend of mine.” But earlier this week, the two appeared in a TikTok video that seems to confirm they’re dating. The clip shows them posing in a photo booth at Chuck E. Cheese.

“The moment vs. the picture,” Siwa wrote on the video, which shows her and Cyrus posing together compared to the sketch-style images that were created. At one point, Siwa and Cyrus share a kiss. In another shot, they gaze at each other while smiling.

Siwa captioned the video, “Happiest girl.” She also recently posted a video of her and Cyrus performing a dance together. She wrote that she was “so proud of her for learning this” and pointed to her “CUTEST HAPPY FACE!!!”

Cyrus Has Also Posted About Their Relationship

Recently, Cyrus posted a TikTok video that only added more fuel to the dating rumors. She shared a screenshot of a text conversation with Siwa, who told her she was sick and needed a hug. Cyrus responded that she was “all the way in Florida.”

She then showed herself putting on a backpack and going through the airport, eventually ending up in a hug with Siwa. Cyrus captioned the video, “Sick = hugs.”

In August, Cyrus confirmed that she had split up with her girlfriend Soph Mosca, who is also a TikTok star. At the time, Mosca wrote, “I’m hopeful that one day we may find our way back to each other.”
