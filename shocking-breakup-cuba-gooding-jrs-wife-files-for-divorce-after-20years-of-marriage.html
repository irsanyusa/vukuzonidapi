Fighting Temptations actor, Cuba Gooding Jr.'s wife has filed for separation from the Oscar winner after 20 years of being married.Gooding's wife, Sara Kapfer filed yesterday in L.A. County Superior Court, citing irreconcilable differences. She and Cuba were high school sweethearts and got married in 1994. They have 3 children, 2 of whom are minors.According to the documents, child support will be determined later but Sara wants joint legal and physical custody.What's funny is the fact that just last year, Cuba gushed about his wife on "The View," talking about how wonderful it is to be married to someone for so long.
