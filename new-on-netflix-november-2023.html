Everything New on Netflix in November 2023

September 05, 2024
blog

As dreary November rolls in, Netflix has a smorgasbord of offerings for the cornucopia. On Nov. 22, High on the Hog: How African American Cuisine Transformed America returns with its second season to explore how African American food has shaped the nation. On Nov. 16, Part 1 of the sixth and final season of The Crown will offer up enough drama to mirror dysfunctional family gatherings around the approaching holiday season. And Rustin, streaming Nov. 17, tells the story of the titular, overlooked Civil Rights leader, making for a good Thanksgiving watch.

Here’s everything coming to Netflix in November 2023—and what’s leaving.
